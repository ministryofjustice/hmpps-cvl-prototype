Check for civil orders

You must include any civil orders in the release pack to give to this person when they leave prison.

For example, this could include a domestic violence protection order and domestic violence protection notice.

How to check for civil orders

You can check:

  • NDelius
  • documents from the court and the CPS

You can also contact:

  • the police
  • the court
  • other agencies such as MAPPA, IOM, children’s services and housing authorities
