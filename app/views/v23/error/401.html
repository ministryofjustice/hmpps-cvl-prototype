If you work for the Probation Service

Access problems can be caused if your details in NDelius are not up to date. Check these details are correct:

  • Role
  • Team or teams if you work in more than one
  • Region
  • Grade
  • PDU

If you work for a prison

Ask a local systems administrator in your office to check you have one of these roles on Digital Prison Services:

  • Licences case admin – for case administrators and others who do not need to approve licences. A local systems administrator can give you this if you need it

  • Licences decision makers – for people who approve licences, including those who provide cover when these people are away. You must request this from helpdesk if you do not have it

For support

Contact the helpdesk by calling 0800 917 5148, or #6598 from inside a prison.
